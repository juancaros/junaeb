This project could not have been posible without the contribution of the Chilean National Board of School Aid (JUNAEB). Their large network of collaborators that allow for data collection and critical insights is central to our work. You can see more of the invaluable work they do here.

Juan Carlos Caro

I am a researcher in Health Economics at the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill. Previously, I have worked on multidimensional poverty, access to public services, food systems and program evaluation. I completed undergraduate and graduate work in economics at the University of Chile. Currently, I am is dedicated to study unfavorable environments, parental behavior, early human capital accumulation and its effects on individual decisions that impact nutritional health, socio-emotional development and health outcomes at large. This work involves close collaboration with ongoing interventions in Africa, Asia and Latin America.
